AI Trading Web Application

Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0 (MVP)

Product Goal: To develop a web-based AI trading application that assists users in making informed trading decisions using AI-driven signals, market analysis, and automated alerts. The system focuses on education, decision support, and risk management rather than blind auto-trading.


---

1. Product Overview

Product Name: TradeMind AI (working title)

Problem Statement: Retail traders often lose money due to lack of market knowledge, emotional trading, and poor risk management. Existing platforms are complex and not beginner-friendly.

Solution: TradeMind AI provides AI-generated trade signals, market insights, and risk-based suggestions through a clean web interface, helping users trade more confidently.

Target Users:

Beginner retail traders

Intermediate traders

Part-time traders

Trading learners / students

5. Functional Requirements

AI Signal Generation Logic

1. Fetch real-time and historical market data.


2. Preprocess data (indicators: RSI, MACD, EMA).


3. AI model predicts market direction.


4. Risk management module calculates Stop Loss & Target.


5. Signal confidence score is generated.


6. Signal displayed to users.




---

Alert Logic

1. User subscribes to a signal.


2. System monitors live price.


3. Alert triggered when entry / SL / target hits.


4. Notification sent via web/email.
